WHO WE ARE

United Diagnostics is an endeavour to excel in providing world-class diagnostics solutions. We take pride in partnering with coveted diagnostic devices brands in the international market and bringing you tomorrow's diagnostics, today.
With the vision of enabling small and medium hospitals/laboratories across cities and towns of india with advanced diagnostic solutions, United Diagnostics aspires to make affordable healthcare accessible for as many lives as possible. We specialise in clinical chemistry analysers which are otherwise perceived to be a cornerstone quality of premium healthcare players and related accessories (filters) used in human and veterinary diagnoses. We are the licensed distribution partners for FujiFilm, a well-known authority in this industry.
